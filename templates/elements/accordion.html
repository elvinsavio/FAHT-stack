Why did the king tax jokes?

The kingdom's treasury ran dry and the king, after much deliberation, decided to tax the one thing his people loved most: laughter.

How much is a joke taxed?

The rate depends on the quality of the joke. A knock-knock joke costs one copper, a pun costs two, and a full stand-up routine requires a royal permit.

What happened to the jesters?

Most fled to neighbouring kingdoms. Those who stayed filed for jester bankruptcy and now perform only mimes, which are yet to be taxed.
